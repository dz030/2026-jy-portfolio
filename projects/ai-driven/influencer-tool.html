The Problem

Spreadsheet Fatigue & Overpriced Tools

The team was stuck in "spreadsheet hell", manual scraping into Google Sheets was visually cluttered and difficult to navigate. Existing tools were overpriced with features we didn't need.

    The mission:

  • UX Overhaul: Replace "uncomfortable" sheets with a simple, readable UI
  • Smart Scaling: Build a custom, lightweight tool without the enterprise cost.

Research: Define MVP

I met with the marketing team and gathered core needs:

Smart Discovery
Follower counts and hashtag sorting
One-Click Outreach
Bulk email and DM templates
Campaign Logic
Grouping influencers for easy management
