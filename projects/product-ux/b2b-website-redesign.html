The Problem

The business needed a scalable B2B website that could:

  • Establish retailer trust through clear company and product information
  • Restrict wholesale pricing and ordering to approved retail customers

However, I initially lacked deep familiarity with retail customer workflows and pain points.

Process

01

Research & Understanding Users

Researched retail users via tutorials on sourcing products, identified common pain points, and interviewed our CEO and Sales Manager.

02

Low-Fidelity Planning

Created simple low-fidelity layouts to define content structure and hierarchy

03

Theme Selection

Evaluated Shopify themes and selected the closest match to the low-fidelity layout to accelerate development

04

Design + Implementation in Parallel

Designed and implemented in Shopify, adding custom code and iterating rapidly.

Reflection

This project taught me how to design and ship under real-world constraints by balancing speed, functionality, and visual quality. Designing directly within Shopify reinforced the importance of aligning UX decisions with implementation feasibility to drive measurable business outcomes.
