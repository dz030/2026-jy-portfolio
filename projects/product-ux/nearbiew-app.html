The Problem

Customers

    Local beauty customers couldn’t:

  • Find nearby store sales
  • Access a trusted community for beauty store reviews
  • Compare local deals in one place

Retailers

    Independent beauty stores lacked:

  • Digital visibility
  • Easy tools to promote local discounts
  • Cost-effective ways to drive foot traffic

Bridging Offline Beauty Retail with Online Discovery

NearBiew connects:

Customers

Discover nearby beauty deals

Retailers

Promote local offers and brands

Why the Project Was Paused

MVP timeline expanded
Initial 3-month MVP grew to ~12 months due to added features and scope complexity.
AI Market shift
Rapid advancement of AI-driven discovery tools reduced the long-term standalone value of the app concept.
Strategic pause
The project was paused before launch to reassess feasibility and market relevance.

Reflection

Early in the project, I prioritized visual polish over implementation speed. This experience taught me when to intentionally simplify visuals, focus on core functionality, and ship an MVP on time to validate the product.
