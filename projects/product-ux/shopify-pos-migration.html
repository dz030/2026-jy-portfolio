The Problem

Two tools. Zero integration. Every sale created duplicate work & errors.

Because QuickBooks and Loyverse operate as separate systems, orders placed in Loyverse had to be manually recreated as invoices in QuickBooks. This resulted in time-consuming workflows, frequent typos, and a higher risk of fulfillment errors across teams.

    This created specific pain points for each team:

  • Sales: Slow and inefficient order placement
  • Warehouse: Time lost recreating invoices and packing slips

What I Built

01

Auto-Invoice Templates

Created auto-generated invoice formats for customer, packing, and special orders.

02

POS UI Design

Set up and designed the POS interface and product listings, using images and logical grouping to improve learnability and speed of order entry.
