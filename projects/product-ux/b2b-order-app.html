The Problem

Retail customers needed a faster way to reorder products when:

  • Sales staff are absent
  • Products are urgently needed
  • Existing competitor apps offered faster workflows

    Challenges included:

  • Tight 3-month deadline
  • Integration with existing Shopify website and POS
  • Maintaining platform consistency without interrupting workflows

Research

Stakeholder Interviews + Competitive Research

Due to the tight 3-month timeline, research was conducted through structured stakeholder interviews with internal sales staff who work directly with retail customers daily.

  • Retail customers were switching to competitor apps when sales staff were unavailable, the business was losing reorders to faster alternatives
  • The existing B2B website required too many steps to reorder, creating friction at the point of urgent need
  • Speed of reorder, not breadth of features, defined the MVP priority

The Solution

2 Ways Quick Ordering

Enabled fast refills through barcode scanning from retailer shelves and one-click reordering from order history.

Barcode Scan > Order
Order History > Reorder

Shopify Integration

Synced products, inventory, customer data, and sales in real time, with all retail activity visible in Shopify analytics.

Workflow Trade-offs

Redirected complex actions (order details, order status variations, and address edits) to the existing B2B website due to real-time data complexity and development constraints.

Original Plan (Order Detail on App)
Trade-off (Order Detail on Web)

Minimal UI

Prioritized usability and ordering speed using default UI patterns over visual customization.

Usability Testing

Round 01

2 rounds of usability testing with our Sales Manager (closest proxy to target users) across 5 tasks using think-aloud protocol. Designs were iterated between rounds based on findings.

Key Iterations

  • Activation: Simplified onboarding steps and clarified confirmation state
  • Quantity Input: Added explicit unit labels and improved button interaction
  • Product Browsing: Sticky image header, full product titles, alphabetical default sort

Round 02

Average 51% faster completion

All 5 tasks improved after targeted design iterations.
