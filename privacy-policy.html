Privacy & Analytics Policy

Last updated: March 2026



How I use data

This portfolio website uses Google Analytics to analyze traffic patterns. This helps me understand which of my design projects are most interesting to visitors and improve the overall user experience.


Data Collected

The analytics data collected is anonymized and includes information such as your browser type, the pages you visit, and the duration of your stay. I do not collect personal identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.


Your Control

You have the right to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking. You can do this by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
